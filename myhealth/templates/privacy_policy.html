Privacy Policy for Secure PHR Management System

Effective Date: 1 March 2025

At Secure PHR Management System (“we,” “our,” or “us”), we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users’ personal health information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard the data you entrust to us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII):
    • Full name, date of birth, gender, contact details
  • Health Information:
    • Medical history, prescriptions, test results, demographic information
  • System Use Data:
    • Login timestamps, device information, and user activity logs
  • Authentication Data:
    • Encrypted passwords and security questions

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Store and manage personal health records
  • Facilitate secure data sharing with authorized healthcare providers
  • Authenticate and authorize users securely
  • Improve system functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements (including HIPAA)

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except in the following situations:

  • With healthcare providers you authorize
  • When legally required (e.g., court orders, law enforcement requests)
  • In case of threats to life or safety where disclosure is legally permissible

4. Security Measures

We take your data privacy seriously and implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Data Encryption: All records are encrypted both in transit and at rest.
  • Authentication: Secure login with multi-factor authentication.
  • Access Controls: Role-based access ensures only authorized individuals can access specific data.
  • Audit Logs: All data access and actions are logged for transparency and monitoring.

5. User Control

You have full control over your health records. You can:

  • View and edit your personal health information
  • Grant or revoke access to specific healthcare providers
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Download your data in a readable format

6. Data Retention

Your data will be retained only as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws. Upon deletion, all data will be permanently removed from our systems after a secure wipe process.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our platform is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

8. Compliance

Secure PHR Management System complies with:

  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be communicated through our platform, and your continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
